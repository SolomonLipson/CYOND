Data Deletion Request

Cyond Engineers App users can request deletion of their personal data at any time.

How to request deletion

Email us at support@cyond.com with the subject Data Deletion Request.

Please include:

  • Your registered mobile number or email used in the app
  • Your full name
  • Any additional details that help us identify your account

What we delete

  • Profile and account identifiers linked to your app account
  • Inspection records and related uploaded media linked to your account (where applicable)

Processing time

We process deletion requests within 90 days of verification.

Some data may be retained only where legally required, for fraud prevention, dispute handling, or security obligations.
