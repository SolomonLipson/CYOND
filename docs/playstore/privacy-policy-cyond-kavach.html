Privacy Policy - CYOND Kavach

Last updated: 20 April 2026

CYOND Kavach ("we", "our", "us") is a home inspection and waterproofing service app used by customers to raise service requests, manage bookings, upload/view service media, track status, and receive notifications.

Information We Collect

  • Account details such as name, phone number, and login verification details (for OTP-based authentication).
  • Service details, ticket details, booking details, and customer communication data.
  • Media and files you upload for service diagnosis (photos, related attachments).
  • Approximate or precise location information when needed for service delivery and booking support.
  • Device and app diagnostics required for app stability, fraud prevention, and troubleshooting.
  • Payment-related references necessary to process and verify transactions.

How We Use Information

  • To create and manage your account and authenticate access to app features.
  • To provide inspection/waterproofing services, booking management, and support workflows.
  • To display uploaded media and service history within your account.
  • To send transactional notifications (for example booking, ticket, and payment updates).
  • To improve reliability, prevent abuse, and maintain platform security.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data may be shared only with authorized internal teams and trusted infrastructure/ service providers strictly for delivering requested services, payment processing, customer support, legal compliance, and security operations.

Data Retention

We retain data only as long as required for service operations, support, compliance, and legal obligations. Data is deleted or anonymized when no longer required, subject to applicable law.

Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational safeguards, including encryption in transit, to protect data.

Your Choices

You may request correction, export, or deletion of your personal data, subject to legal and operational limitations.
