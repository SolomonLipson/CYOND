Privacy Policy - Cyond Engineers App

Last updated: 17 March 2026

Cyond Engineers App ("we", "our", "us") is used by field engineers to perform inspection, capture measurements, upload media, and generate service reports.

Information We Collect

  • Account and profile data required for login and role access.
  • Inspection data, comments, and measurement details entered by engineers.
  • Media files (photos/drawings) uploaded during inspection workflows.
  • Location data when required for appointment check-in and location validation.
  • Device/network diagnostics used for stability and troubleshooting.

How We Use Information

  • To provide inspection workflow features and generate reports/quotations/checklists.
  • To sync lead, appointment, and feedback data with our backend systems.
  • To maintain service quality, security, and auditability of field operations.

Data Retention

Security
