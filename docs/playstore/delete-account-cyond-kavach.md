# CYOND Kavach - Account and Data Deletion Request

Last updated: 20 April 2026

This page applies to **CYOND Kavach** by **Cyond Business Solution Pvt Ltd**.

Users can request deletion of their account and associated data at any time.

## How to request account deletion

Please send an email to **support@cyond.com** with the subject line:

`Delete CYOND Kavach Account`

Include the following so we can verify your account safely:

- Registered mobile number used in CYOND Kavach
- Registered email address (if available)
- Full name
- Optional screenshot of profile page (if available)

## What will be deleted

After verification, we delete or anonymize data associated with your account, including:

- Account profile details
- Booking and ticket history linked to your account
- Uploaded service media linked to your account (where applicable)
- Device tokens used for notifications

## What may be retained and why

Some data may be retained for a limited period only when required for:

- Legal or regulatory compliance
- Fraud prevention and security investigations
- Financial reconciliation and dispute handling

## Retention period

- Account deletion requests are processed within **30 business days** after verification.
- Compliance-related records, if required, may be retained for up to **180 days** and then deleted/anonymized, unless a longer legal retention period applies.
